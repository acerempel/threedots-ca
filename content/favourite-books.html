My favourite works of fiction in book-form are these (in order of when I read them, starting with the most recent):

  1. Lincoln in the Bardo by George Saunders.
  2. Station Eleven by Emily St John Mandel.
  3. Manhattan Beach by Jennifer Eagan.
  4. The Lathe of Heaven by Ursula LeGuin.
  5. The Life and Opinions of Tristram Shandy by Laurence Sterne.
